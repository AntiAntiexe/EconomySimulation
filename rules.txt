Household
- Have 3 resources
    - Land ->
    - Labour
    - Capital
- Sell resources for income to the firms
- They use income to purchase goods and services from firms
- The success of a household is measured by the amount of goods and services

Firms
- Start with set amount of money
- Use money to purchase resources
- If 1 of each resource is owned, trade for goods and service
- They can sell their goods and services for money
- The success of a firm is measured by the amount of profit they have gained or lost
